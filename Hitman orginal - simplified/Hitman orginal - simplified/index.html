about us

Hitman Academy run by Head Instructor Cole Henning Aka "The Hitman". A Martial Arts Academy where anyone can join and feel at home. Allowing those who need to train to get fit, loose weight, learn Self-defence,improve their techniques or become a competitive fighter. We offer classes for kids and adults that build and improve their Self-Confidence, Strength, Fitness, Co-Ordination, Self-Control and discipline. We strive to give our students the tools to grow as individuals. We compete in Kickboxing, BJJ, MMA and Krav maga competitions as well as host and grade our students in their choosen discipline

Our Classes

MMA

Mixed Martial Arts ,one of the fastest growing sports. We will teach you the technical elements of striking, grappling and jui-jitsu from active and competing athletes

Cardio Kickboxing

A great way to get fit and kick some butt! In our intense cardio kickboxing class designed to burn calories, push your endurance and burn fat
