about us

Hitman Academy is a dream of Cole Henning (Head Instructor & Owner) This is a place where anyone can join and feel at home. Allowing those who need to train to get fit, loose weight, learn Self-defence or come to improve their techniques to become an elite fighter either in the ring or cage. We offer classes for kids and adults that build and improve their Self-Confidence, Strength, Fitness, Co-Ordination, Self-Control and discipline. We strive to give our students the tools to grow as an individual. We Participate in Kickboxing, BJJ, MMA and Krav maga gradings and competitions allowing our students to work towards a goal of mastery. our students can be awarded Gauteng/SA colours in Kickboxing & MMA and with enough merit travel over-seas to compete.

Our Classes

MMA

Mixed Martial Arts ,one of the fastest growing sports. We will teach you the technical elements of striking, grappling and jui-jitsu from active and competing athletes

Cardio Kickboxing

A great way to get fit and kick some butt! In our intense cardio kickboxing class designed to burn calories, push your endurance and burn fat

Krav Maga

Israeli self defense system ,a no bars held system covering situational awareness,weapons and effecient street fighting tactics

Kids Kickboxing

A fun self defense/antibulling class to teach your child discpline and build their character through art of kickboxing

Functional Training

Geared towards strength core endurance and stability, an overall great way to get fit.

Private Lessons
